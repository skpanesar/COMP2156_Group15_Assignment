In devops course students can gain knowledge of the best practices, protocols, and workflows that a DevOps engineer implements in the workplace to boost output in this course. Utilise an industry-standard toolbox to conduct practical work while analysing and assessing the many developer operation implementations that are available.
Develop a solution for a challenging business issue while upholding the fundamental duties of DevOps.Along with DevOps tools like Ansible and Git, students will learn about data formats and types such as JavaScript Object Notation (JSON), YAML (Yaml Ain't Markup Language), YANG (Yet Another Next Generation), and their use in network automation.
